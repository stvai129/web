Male Cows and sometimes female cows too are used for carrying carts. In Harappan Civilization, A well equipped Bullock cart was used for transportation and farming. Still, in various villages of India, carts are used for farming.

Cow Dung is also very useful. It is used as manure. In rural India, cow dung is used for household fuel, as insect repellents, etc.
